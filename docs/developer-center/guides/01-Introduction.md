## Introduction

Use these guides to learn about integrating CARTOframes into your **data science** workflows. Working inside of a Jupyter notebook, the guides walk through initial set-up and installation, reading data from CARTO into your Python session, writing data to your CARTO account, and the basics of visualizing your data on a map.
